Services

Custom made circuits

We do circuit designing, manufacture and assembly of parts. Custom enclosures can be designed and 3D printed based on the footprint of the circuit if needed.

Home Automation

Expect anything from a simple automatic on/off switch to a full home automation. Control your appliances with Wi-Fi, motion control or a automatic timer. Or maybe recode your TV remote to control it.

CNC Engraving

We engrave names and designs on wood boards or flat plastics that suit custom gifts.
